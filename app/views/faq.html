Frequently Asked Questions

How do you achieve status with the airlines?
You can achieve status by choosing one airline, signing up for their miles program, and then sticking with it. The more you fly, the higher your status and the more rewards and benefits you will receive.
How can I get help if my plane cancels?
Call us. Your personal Cet Services agent will work with you immediately to help you find another flight.
How can I get rewards with the hotels I stay in?
Cet Services does not keep or discount your points. Instead, we can help you set up a rewards program with the hotel chain of your choice, giving you the most amount of points and rewards for each stay. We can help you compare and choose the program that is best for you.
